football betting

When it comes to the worldwide popularity of online sports betting, no sport can compete with football.

Most countries in the world play football and have national teams, making football a well-known sport. To put it into perspective, there are more countries affiliated with FIFA than with the United Nations.

The beautiful game

Football is called the "beautiful game" because it gives you a feeling of joy and passion, and betting on football is no different.

If you already love watching football, you will find that betting on your favourite football team will only make it more thrilling and entertaining.

Many fans like to follow certain players and teams and know them well enough to understand their strengths and weaknesses, which allows them to make better decisions when it comes to betting on the winners of football matches and tournaments.

As long as you stick to what you know, there is loads of fun betting to be had on the thousands of football matches played worldwide.

Specials in football

bwin will provide you odds on the probability of the outcome of matches and events within matches, such as the number of goals scored and individual scorers.

The winner of the match bet is the simplest form of football betting in which you predict the outcome of a match. You can bet on the home team to win, the away team to win, or a draw. It is important to note that match bets are settled on the result after 90 minutes. Thus, if the scores are equal, a draw becomes the winning bet —regardless of the result after extra time or penalties.

Daily specials on combi + and price boost are competitive odds to try and give you the best bet. Throughout your football betting experience, there are several different types of bets, including accumulators which combine various selections to make a mix of odds on which to place your bet.

The best football competitions to bet on

There are many football competitions such as the World Cup, Euro Cup, the five major European league championships, and the Champions League.

The best players in the world and the best European teams compete in the Champions League every year with the ultimate aim of winning the tournament, the prize money and automatic qualification for next season.
